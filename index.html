Poorly Drawn Heroes

About Our Heroes

Eric is a an unexpected dinosaur who wears glasses, a rustic hat, and a purple necktie. He is a dinosaur who was created by Reza Farazmond. I first discovered Poorly Drawn Lines while on a trip to San Francisco. I happened to wander into a bookstore, where I noticed the first book since it was features as a new book by a local author. I picked it up and could not put it down, so I bought it on the spot. I've become a huge fan and followed the strip ever since.

Some characters you'll meet.

Will you meet a bear from outer space? Likely. How about a bird of unknown origin who is the bear's best friend? Also likely. You may also meet some aliens, some cats, a dog or two. You never know. Here's some of the characters you might meet, should you decide to take a trip into the world of Poorly Drawn Lines.

  • Ladybugs of all kinds.
  • A misunderstood cobra.
  • A beautiful mountain.
  • Smack-talking Robots.
